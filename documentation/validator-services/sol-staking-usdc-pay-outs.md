# SOL staking (USDC pay-outs)

Triton runs a Private Trusted Validator that pays your staking rewards back in USDC, once a week, so you never have to unstake or convert before you can spend them.

## The problem with most staking

On Solana you can run your own validator, delegate to someone else's, swap into a liquid staking token (LST), or provide DeFi liquidity. Each generates rewards, but spending them is rarely simple: native staking means splitting and unstaking part of your delegation first, and LSTs can create a taxable event every time you trade or convert.

## How the Private Validator works

You delegate SOL to a private validator that runs at 100% commission and distributes the rewards back to you in USDC every week. The payout combines:

* staking rewards from Solana's native inflation,
* part of the block rewards, and
* MEV tips via Jito.

## Who it's for

* Teams with larger delegations (minimum 2,500 SOL) who want steady income to cover operating expenses.
* Delegators in regions where converting staking rewards into stablecoins adds tax complexity, where receiving rewards directly in USDC is a smoother path.

Triton is a long-standing trusted validator that participates in Solana governance and invests heavily in failover and reliability.
